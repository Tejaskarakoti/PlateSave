About PlateSave

Our Mission

PlateSave was born from a simple yet powerful realization: while millions of people in India go to bed hungry every night, tons of perfectly good food end up in landfills daily. This paradox inspired Team Hack Horizon to create a technology solution that could bridge this gap.

During our research, we discovered that 68 million tonnes of food are wasted annually in India, while 194 million people remain undernourished. The problem wasn't the lack of food, but the lack of an efficient system to connect surplus food with those who need it most.

Built for the SIH Internal Hackathon 2025 under the theme "Agriculture and Food Technology / Clean and Green," PlateSave represents our commitment to using technology for social good. We believe that every meal saved is a step towards ending hunger and building a more sustainable future.

"Technology should serve humanity. PlateSave is our contribution to a world where no one sleeps hungry while food goes to waste."
